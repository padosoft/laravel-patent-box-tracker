# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

This project adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) version 2.1 verbatim.

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation. We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Enforcement

Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behaviour may be reported to the project team at `lorenzo.padovani@padosoft.com`. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
